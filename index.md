## About Me

<img class="profile-picture" src="chl.jpg">

I am currently a second year master student in pattern recognition and intelligent system at [Beihang University](https://www.buaa.edu.cn). My supervisor is Prof. [Baochang Zhang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=WH0J_34AAAAJ&hl=en). Now I'm an intern in Alibaba DAMO Academy, and my mentor is Xiuyu Sun. My research interests are in machine learning and computer vision, especially in neural architecture search (NAS), model quantization and pruning, unsupervised learning and adversarial deep learning.
